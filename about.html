Vision

SM College aims to promote the public welfare by exercising an influence on behalf of humanity and civilization, fostering the development of knowledge and the pursuit of truth.

Mission

SM's mission is to promote the public welfare by exercising an influence on behalf of humanity and civilization, through the education of students and the advancement of knowledge in various fields, with a commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion. The college seeks to prepare students to be leaders and innovators who will contribute positively to society.
